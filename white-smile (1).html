Is dental treatment painful at White Smile?

+

Absolutely not. We use the most advanced comfort systems available, including computer-controlled anesthesia delivery and dental sedation options. Our team is trained in pain management, and the vast majority of our patients report feeling nothing at all during procedures.

How long do dental implants last?

+

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium post integrates permanently with your jawbone, while the ceramic crown typically lasts 15–25 years before needing replacement. We offer a 10-year structural guarantee on all implants placed at our clinic.

What results can I expect from whitening?

+

Our laser whitening system delivers an average of 8–10 shades of improvement in a single 60-minute session. Results vary depending on your baseline shade and tooth composition, but we offer a colour-match consultation beforehand so you know exactly what to expect. Results typically last 2–3 years with proper maintenance.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

+

New patients are typically seen within 3–5 business days for consultations. For urgent care, we reserve same-day appointments. Our online booking system allows you to select your preferred time slot instantly, 24 hours a day.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

+

Yes. We offer 0% interest financing for up to 24 months through our partnered lenders, as well as in-house payment plans. We work with most major insurance providers and our treatment coordinators will maximize your coverage before any out-of-pocket estimate is given.
